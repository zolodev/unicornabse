Quickstart

A great example where to start is to take a look at password policies. As of today year 2022, we need at least 14 characters to make a strong password.

So if you need a quickfix, start with these two: 1. Remove password retention polices (not forcing users to change passwords) 2. All users should have at least 14 characters of lenght.

The rest of the information in this guide will be added over time. This guide is a work in progress and will be a subject to change.
